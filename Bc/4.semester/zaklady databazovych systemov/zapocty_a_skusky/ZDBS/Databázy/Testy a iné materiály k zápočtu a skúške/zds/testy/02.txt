Ahoj, nemohl by me nekdo prosim ukazat jak by resil tenhle priklad v Sql 

Kino (IdK,Kapacita), Film (IdF,Reziser), Prog(IdK,IdF,Termin)  - klice Idk v 
Kino , IdF ve Film a vsechno v Prog 


Filmy s nejvetsi potencionalni navstevnosi (nejvetsi suma kapacit) 



Predem diky 

===

select IdF from Prog natural join Kino group by IdF
having sum(Kapacita)>=all(select sum(Kapacita) from Prog natural join Kino group by IdF)

misto "Prog natural join Kino" muze byt "Prog,Kino where Prog.IdF=Kino.IdF"

Venca

===

Mozna neco jako: 

select sum(Kino.Kapacita) from Kino, Prog where Kino.Idk = Prog.Idk group by Prog.Idf
